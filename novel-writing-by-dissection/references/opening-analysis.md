# Opening Analysis — 开篇拆解 (Chapters 1-3)

## Chapter 1: 黑暗恐惧

### Structure
1. **Disorientation** (paragraphs 1-4): Wakes up, memory loss, 3 days post-transmigration
2. **Physical struggle** (paragraphs 5-10): Head injury, sits up, feels scar
3. **Atmospheric setup** (paragraphs 11-18): Darkness, inexplicable fear, explores the house
4. **World glimpse** (paragraphs 19-28): Opens door, sees the village, poverty confirmed
5. **Return to darkness** (paragraphs 29-35): Closes door, fear returns, psychological vs supernatural ambiguity
6. **Cliffhanger** (final paragraph): Door opens by itself

### Techniques Used
- **Sensory-first world-building**: Describe what protagonist sees/touches, not facts
- **False rational explanation**: Protagonist tries to explain fear as PTSD
- **Pacing**: Slow exploration, then sudden crisis at chapter end
- **Limited perspective**: Reader only knows what protagonist knows

### Word Count: ~2200 characters (standard web novel chapter length)

## Chapter 2: 阴鬼

### Structure
1. **Immediate crisis continuation**: Door opens, silence, no footsteps
2. **Invisible attack**: Hands choke protagonist, cannot be touched/seen
3. **Near-death climax**: Vision blurs, almost dies
4. **Rescue**: Parents arrive, drive away the threat
5. **Explanation**: "阴鬼" introduced, partial world-building
6. **Ambiguous resolution**: Parents act as if this is normal → unsettling

### Horror Techniques
- **Invisible enemy**: Cannot fight what cannot be seen
- **Ineffective resistance**: Protagonist's martial training (from前世) useless
- **Casual normalization**: Parents treat near-death experience as routine

## Chapter 3: 小灯符

### Structure
1. **Aftermath**: Sore throat, parents explain more
2. **New rule**: 小灯符 as protection
3. **Symbol introduction**: The灯符 becomes recurring protective item
4. **Suspicion**: Protagonist's police instincts question parents' story
5. **End hook**: Something still feels wrong

### Information Delivery
- Not "the world has ghosts" — instead: "here's a talisman to protect you"
- Reader learns through objects and actions
- Protagonist's skepticism mirrors reader's questions

## Takeaway for New Novels

The first 3 chapters establish:
- **Protagonist personality**: cautious, analytical, observant
- **Core threat**: invisible supernatural dangers
- **First tool**: 小灯符 (protection)
- **Bigger mystery**: What is this world? Why is he here?
- **Tone**: realistic psychological response to supernatural horror
