# Source Map

Use this file when the task needs concrete places to look for research-backed or news-triggered topic candidates.

## Technology Sources

- `arXiv`: best for early signals, especially AI, ML, systems, and alignment
- `conference proceedings`: NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ACL, CVPR, KDD, SIGGRAPH, CHI, FAccT
- `lab and company research blogs`: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, Microsoft Research, Meta AI, Nvidia Research
- `benchmark and eval reports`: model cards, leaderboard reports, safety evaluations, systems benchmarks

## Economics Sources

- `working papers`: NBER, CEPR, SSRN, central bank working paper series
- `journals`: AER, QJE, JPE, Econometrica, Review of Economic Studies
- `institutional reports`: IMF, World Bank, OECD, BIS, Fed, ECB, BoE
- `data releases`: CPI, PPI, payrolls, unemployment, GDP, PMIs, industrial production, trade balances

## Good Topic Patterns

Look for one of these shapes:

- a popular claim outruns the actual evidence
- a technical result changes economic behavior or incentives
- a policy change reveals an underlying mechanism
- a paper explains a headline more clearly than the headline itself
- several recent events can all be unified by one principle

## Bad Topic Patterns

Avoid or downgrade topics when:

- the finding is only incremental
- the mechanism cannot be explained without heavy jargon
- the paper matters only inside a narrow subfield
- the topic depends entirely on hype with no primary-source support

## Fast Rating Grid

Score each candidate from 1 to 5:

| Factor | What to ask |
| --- | --- |
| Relevance | Will the intended audience care soon? |
| Explanation value | Is there a real mechanism to unpack? |
| Transfer value | Can this principle explain other cases too? |
| Timeliness | Is there an active discussion window? |
| Differentiation | Can the angle be more original than summary coverage? |
| Evidence strength | Are there solid primary sources? |

## Suggested Deliverable Shapes

- `5-10 ranked ideas`: best for an open ideation request
- `3 deeply developed ideas`: best for immediate production planning
- `1 topic, 3 formats`: best for article/thread/video repackaging
- `1 week slate`: best for consistent publishing cadence
